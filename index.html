Selected research

My doctoral work under Frank Wood (PLAI group) focused on structured amortized variational inference and the role of explicit structure in learning systems. The following papers are representative outcomes of that line of work:

  • Graphically Structured Diffusion Models — generative models with explicit structural priors
  • Flexible Diffusion Modeling of Long Videos — large-scale generative modeling
  • All-in-One Simulation-Based Inference — unifying amortized and online inference
  • Daphne: Multi-Pass Compilation of Probabilistic Programs — compiling probabilistic programs into inference graphs
  • Decoupling Conflict Resolution with CDVCS — versioned, conflict-aware replication
